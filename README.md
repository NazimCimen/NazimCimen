## Featured Product — Lingzy

> **Read World Classics Without the Struggle.**

[Lingzy](https://www.lingzyapp.com) is an English learning app I designed, built, and shipped independently. It lets learners read timeless literature in their original form while dynamically adapting the text to their exact proficiency level — bridging the gap between oversimplified graded readers and inaccessible classic prose.

**Core Features**
- **Instant Level Adaptation** — Any paragraph can be rewritten on-demand to match the reader's current level, preserving the author's voice while reducing friction.
- **Smart Tap Dictionary** — Inline word lookup with meaning, translation, and native pronunciation without leaving the reading flow.
- **AI Story Generator** — Personalized story creation using AI to reinforce vocabulary in context.
- **Word Workshop** — Spaced-repetition vocabulary practice derived from the user's reading history.
- **World Classics Library** — Curated collection of public-domain literature, available for free.
